Analyzing the Potential Relationship between the Presence of Starbucks and the Gentrification of an Area

Nikhila Iyer

December 01, 2021

Background + Problem Statement

Background

Starbucks

Starbucks is a multinational coffeehouse chain headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Culturally, Starbucks has become a staple within society, but a cup of coffee at Starbucks is not affordable for everyone.

Gentrification

Gentrification is when more affluent residents and businesses move into a low socioeconomic area.

Leads to:

  • increase in property values of the area
  • increase in median household income
  • increase in percent of residents with a college degree
  • population migration and displacement

Problem Statement

Is there a relationship between number of Starbucks stores in an area and the level of gentrification?
